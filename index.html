Iris FLower Classification

This project involves using machine learning techniques to classify the Iris dataset, a well-known dataset in the field of machine learning and statistics. The goal is to predict the species of Iris flowers based on four features: sepal length, sepal width, petal length, and petal width. The Iris dataset consists of 150 samples, divided into three classes of 50 samples each, representing three species of Iris flowers: Setosa, Versicolor, and Virginica.
